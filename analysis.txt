OwO Bot - HuntBot Mathematical Progression Analysis

1. Core Concept: The "Cheapest Next" ROI Strategy

In typical idle-style progression games like OwO's HuntBot, your upgrades (traits) scale in two ways:
* Linear Benefits: Your animals per hour, essence per hour, and idle duration scale up in roughly flat, predictable increments.
* Exponential Costs: The Animal Essence required to reach the next level multiplies rapidly.

Because the cost of upgrading heavily outpaces the flat benefit you gain per level, the most mathematically optimal approach to maximizing hourly yield is a balanced portfolio. Sinking 400,000 essence into a single level of `Cost` while leaving `Efficiency` at level 1 starves the account of its ability to generate the essence needed to progress.

To solve this, this automation tool implements a dynamic Return On Investment (ROI) solver:
Instead of prioritizing one specific trait blindly, the script continuously looks at the exact essence required to unlock the *very next level* of all active traits. It automatically purchases the cheapest one available. It repeats this process until all essence is optimally spent. This ensures all traits grow in tandem, maximizing hourly rates while naturally reaching the thresholds to afford expensive upgrades.

2. Trait Roles and Priority

By default, the script groups traits into "Core" (highest ROI) and "Secondary" (ignored unless specified).

CORE TRAITS:
* Efficiency (Max Lvl 215): Increases animals caught per hour. Highly valuable for raw essence generation through sacrifices.
* Duration (Max Lvl 235): Increases the maximum AFK limit for the bot. Crucial for generating income while asleep or offline.
* Gain (Max Lvl 200): Provides flat, raw essence per hour. Acts as an excellent snowball multiplier.
* Cost (Max Lvl 5): Reduces the Cowoncy cost per run. While the final levels are extremely expensive, the dynamic ROI solver ensures it gets leveled up exactly when your other traits become more expensive than it.

SECONDARY TRAITS:
* Experience (Max Lvl 200): Only increases your global OwO level faster. Does not provide direct economic or essence benefit. Disabled by default, but can be forced via CLI `--traits experience`.
* Radar (Max Lvl 999): Marginally increases the chance of dropping Bot Animals. Costs hundreds of billions of essence to max out. Strictly an end-game essence sink. Disabled by default.

3. Algorithm Flow (Bulk Purchase Simulation)

To prevent Discord API rate-limiting (spamming 50 essence 30 times in a row):
1. State Extraction: The script reads the `owo hb` embed to determine the exact level and partial XP (e.g., `[45000/100000]`) of all traits.
2. Memory Simulation: The script plays out the entire progression loop in RAM, deducting essence from a virtual wallet and adding it to the cheapest virtual traits.
3. Batch Execution: Once the virtual wallet is empty, it summarizes the required essence per trait and fires a maximum of one command per trait (e.g., `owo upgrade efficiency 45200`).
